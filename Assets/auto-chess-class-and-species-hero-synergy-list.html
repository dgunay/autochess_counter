Dota Auto Chess: class and species Hero Synergy list

Auto ChessVincenzo "Skulz" MilellaJanuary 30, 2019Guides, Heroes6 Comments

This list shows the Dota Auto Chess heroes organized by class and species. It indicates synergies, gold cost, and chess pieces required. The list is updated periodically.

I have spent time studying the best layout for this synergy list as many Auto Chess players do not play also Dota 2. In the end, I have chosen to display each hero with their name and silhouette icon (the standard portraits might have confused newcomers).

  • The heroes are split according to their class and species.

  • The number prior each name is the gold cost of that particular hero.

  • The bolded numbers specify how many chess pieces are necessary to gain a particular class or species bonus.

I have included all the fundamental info to find out quickly the bonuses provided by a certain class and species, the heroes required, and their gold cost. Hopefully, this page will be a helpful resource for your games :).

Currently, Auto Chess is immensely popular, and I believe there will be several adjustments in the coming months, so I will try my best to keep this list updated with all the changes and new heroes.

Latest update: February 5th, 2019
